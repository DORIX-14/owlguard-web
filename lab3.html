LAB ID: 03

Phishing Analysis

Analyzing suspicious emails and malicious headers to track attackers.

Objective

Is lab ka main goal hai suspicious emails ko investigate karna, unke hidden metadata (headers) ko analyze karna aur sender ki asli identity (IP Address) ka pata lagana.

Required Tools

MXToolbox Messageheader (Google) VirusTotal Whois.domaintools

Analysis Procedure

  • Step 1: Extract Header: Apne email account mein suspicious mail ko open karein. "Show Original" ya "View Header" par click karke sara text copy karein.
  • Step 2: Trace Sender: Google Messageheader tool par header paste karein. Check karein 'Delay' kahan ho raha hai aur sender ka original IP address kya hai.
  • Step 3: Check SPF/DKIM/DMARC: Ye teen parameters batayenge ki email spoofed hai ya real. Agar ye "Fail" hain, toh email 100% fake hai.
  • Step 4: URL Inspection: Email mein maujood links ko direct click na karein. URL ko VirusTotal par scan karein malicious scripts ke liye.

Key Headers to Check

Dhyan dein in important parameters par jab aap forensic analysis karein:

X-Original-IP: [Sender ka actual IP]
Return-Path: [Agar email fail hui toh kahan jayegi]
Reply-To: [Agar sender ka email alag hai Return-Path se]
SAFETY WARNING: Kabhi bhi suspicious attachments (.exe, .zip) ko apne personal computer par download ya open na karein. Hamesha Virtual Machine (VM) ya Sandbox environment ka use karein.
